Hacking the Matrix

Neo is trying to hack the Matrix. He has a list of codes, but to solve this challenge, he must do the following: if the index is even, take the first element, otherwise take the last one. Please print the result to help Neo hack the Matrix.


Sample Input:
1 2 3 8 10 10

Sample Output:
1 10 2 10 3 8



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 8 seconds
